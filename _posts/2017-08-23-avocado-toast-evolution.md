<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Sure, avocado toast is getting a bad rep from all of the Instagrammable toppings and brunching ladies who can’t help but snap 300 overhead shots before everyone is allowed to take a bite. But what’s not to love about arguably the most delicious <i>fruit</i> (yes, avocados are technically fruits), mashed with toppings and loaded on top of crispy toast? Instead of focusing on the “basic” nature of this food, let’s have a look at the remarkable evolution of avocado toast and just how wild this dish got its start.
</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>It all started with four triangles and some chili flakes...</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/7uNc7qUQA8gYaYcO6g4qg4/0361ff86ce11968d0f1b25ae5ede5e7e/GettyImages-502850069.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="635" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>Then people began to get fancy with more spices (like pepper), a squeeze of lemon, and some sprouts...</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/4cnWjy0OpWs2eguik2wUcc/21e564d39b094c789df5de0d9d491305/GettyImages-724292981.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="510" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>And then the most amazing thing happened — someone #PutAnEggOnIt.</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/7vX902eZLqeeQQMq6KMwmc/0255e8c503768a4b6f27fa5614cbd11d/giphy.gif?fit=thumb&amp;q=75" width="500" height="281" />
<p class="listicleItem-title"><strong>Not just a poached egg, but all *kinds* of eggs...</strong></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/4ozrMf4p8kS2EQK4ASqgCS/1fb646e7ac0a0d40c6cbee34a8f6b174/GettyImages-651550898.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="535" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>Some even started topping it with meat!</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/2AEapPhBEQ0Imw4QO2qQE4/80e808e0e81b3f4ca763dd5233f41a9a/GettyImages-575510791.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="592" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>And then things got even crazier. Cuisines adapted the avocado toast, like this Mexican style here…</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/40XneddPDiOaS6MWig4Kcg/00a14246975a01098c295821e676f3b6/GettyImages-565794577.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="596" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>Or this Mediterranean style here…</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/2znWJfm0nO6WIoo4Gq0OSI/2d88b6c0f82422c2c5c1b1a7aa24342f/GettyImages-688865181.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="535" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>And then we departed from cuisines and moved into a much stronger creative force…</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/6HUIkP39cIs0UYUgciAeaa/7bac3220872a200347ab29697a17480c/GettyImages-804788770.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="528" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>And everything started combining into one massive tower of avocado greatness…</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/NEOWJupMWYiameQEwg08y/d069efad926e8da3afc2264867af8cb4/GettyImages-695527056.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="525" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>And ended up as a singular piece of art (with edible flowers of course).</b></span></p>
<img class="size-medium aligncenter" src="https://images.contentful.com/ilg1c26embws/6eVqZ6VaaQcI2GgewcyGQU/9744df1b4da71b06d6595e2525226e76/Fancy_Aco.jpg?fit=thumb&amp;q=75&amp;w=776" width="776" height="731" />
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">In conclusion, avocado toast went from a simple breakfast meal to a stunning (and still delicious) work of art — so can we blame the Instagrammers of today for making it their muse?</span></p>
