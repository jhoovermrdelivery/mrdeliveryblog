&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400;">At this point, you might be starting to like Mr. Delivery as much as we do, which is great, because there’s rewards for that!</span>

&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400;">If you are saying to yourself, “I use Mr. Delivery so much, they should pay me for a sponsorship,” give us a call and we might think about it. You never know if you don’t ask. </span>

<img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/HqUxwOJetL0ha/source.gif" />

&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400;">Without our loyal Mr. D fanbase, we would not be around to change lives with fast delivery and hot and ready food. Which is why we give back through a rewards system that offers us a chance to say thank you. What kind of rewards you ask? Points! </span>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-530" src="http://www.themrdeliveryblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/image17.png" alt="" width="1770" height="900" />

<span style="font-weight: 400;">You’ll receive one point per dollar you spend. These points will add up and give you the ability to redeem them for reward credits towards future orders. For every 250 points $5 credit will get automatically applied on your account. </span>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-534" src="http://www.themrdeliveryblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/image10.png" alt="" width="1999" height="995" />

<span style="font-weight: 400;">When you go to checkout, you can view how many points you will receive with each order. To view your total reward points, visit your account and click the rewards tab to see how many you have so you can reach 250.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-532" src="http://www.themrdeliveryblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/image39.png" alt="" width="640" height="1136" />Once you reach 250 points, your next order will be automatically have the $5 dollar reward credit and cannot be saved, but once the credit is applied, you can keep spending until you earn your next 250 points. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us by clicking the rewards tab, selecting “click here to redeem rewards, and choosing the appropriate tab to relay your question to us.</span>
